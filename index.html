About Us

Western Constructions

Western Group is dedicated to being the leading provider of Real Estate Services and Solutions. Since its founding in 1989, Western Group has grown to a leadership position in the Real Estate Industry. With proven technology, a history of steady growth and successful ventures, Western Group has proven to be an invaluable partner to the people.

Western Group is celebrating more than thirty years of providing the most innovative and environmental design solutions. Its reputation is built upon a comprehensive line of real estate needs satisfying all segments of customers. Western Group is located at Hyderabad in India and an ever-expanding global network of customers abroad.
