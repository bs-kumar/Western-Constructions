About Western Aqua

Our goal for Western Aqua is to create the perfect home for a modern business. Bringing that goal to life has meant finding an outstanding location, assembling the ideal team, and striving for perfection in all aspects of the building's design and execution Creating that space meant designing not just for the present but also for the future. From highly functional design to stunning contemporary architecture, from robust service amenities to state-of-the-art sustainability features, every element of Western Aqua has been designed to make each day more inspired and more productive. Because success tomorrow must be designed today.
