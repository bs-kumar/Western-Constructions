About Western Exotica

Western Exotica is located in Kondapur in shilpa park near Maharshi Marg Vidya Mandir. The project is spread over 3.234 acres of land with 2B+G+7 floors structure consisting of a total of 270 units. Western Exotica offers 2 BHK & 3 BHK Premium apartments with areas 1290 sft – 1473 sft 2 BHK, 1849 sft – 2813 sft 3BHK facing East, West, North.

Specifications

Western exotica doesn’t compromise on the quality and provides world class specification for your homes in premium style. Be it windows or doors to safety and security, all are uniquely built to meet your taste that enhances the standards of living in a gated community.

STRUCTURE

  • R.C.C. framed structure designed to withstand Earthquake Loads with Temperature resistant ‘Aerocon’ Blocks for reduced heat radiation indoors.

FINISHES

  • Internal: Spray Plaster based Smooth finish to internal walls & premium eco-friendly non- volatile paintings.
  • External: Texture finish in Exterior Grade Emulsion (eco-friendly) paints.

DOORS & WINDOWS

  • Main Door: Designer Frame in Teak Wood with Flush Shutters (Melamine/Matt polish) fixed with Brush finished SS Hardware, night latch & safety chain, etc.
  • Internal Doors: Teak/Hard Wood frame with Polished Flush Shutters and Brush finished SS Hardware.
  • Balcony Doors: Two Track French Windows in UPVC with Sliding Doors in Fenesta/ Rehau / LG make.
  • Windows: Three track UPVC Windows with mosquito mesh shutter of Fenesta/ Rehau / LG make and aesthetical safety grills.

FLOORING

  • Wooden: Drawing / Living and Master Bed Rooms.
  • Vitrified Tiles: Gloss finish 80 X 80 cm size for Dinning & Living areas and Standard size for other Bed Rooms
  • Anti-Skid Flooring: Designer Type Vitrified Tiles for Kitchen and Bathrooms, with Glazed Tiles Dadoo up to 7’- 0 ht in Bathrooms and 2’-0 ht over Polished Granite Kitchen Counter Top.
  • Common Areas: Flame Finish Granite / Imported Marble / Super Gloss flooring for Stairs, corridors & lobbies.

PLUMBING & SANITARY

  • Designer type wall mounted WCs and Wash Basins with chrome plated, heavy body metal faucets & fittings, Stainless Steel Cockroach trap & Single Lever fittings in Master Bathroom.
  • Hot water connections to the shower and wash basin. All internal water lines in non PVC, Galvanized metal pipes to avoid internal scaling.
  • Stainless steel sink with drain board in Kitchen with Dish washer and washing machine provision in the utility area.
  • Piped Gas connection through Metered Gas Bank.

ELECTRICAL

  • Concealed Copper Wiring with designer switches. Special provisions for concealed ducting of split air conditioners.
  • 100 % DG Power back with separate meters.
  • Intercom Ports in halls & all bedrooms and kitchen and TV outlets in bedrooms, living / family and drawing rooms.
  • Wi-Fi facility Through Multiple Service Providers.

COMMON AREA FACILITIES

  • No down time at Lifts with 4 Nos. of International quality lifts of 8 and 16 passenger’s capacities in each block.
  • Smart Club House with swimming pool, gym, dinning and guest accommodation.
  • Separate Play Area for Kids, Sport Areas for the young and Recreation Spots for the old.
  • Round the clock Security System with intercom to security, club house and all services located within the premises and Solar fencing.
  • Fire Fighting systems as per regulations with fire detection and alarm public address plus automatic sprinkler system at basements.
