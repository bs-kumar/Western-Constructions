WESTERN PEARL

About Western Pearl

Western pearl is located in the city’s flourishing business corridor, the business centre in Hitech is like no other. With sophisticated décor, this state-of-the-art business office space is designed for maximum productivity. From social hubs to meeting rooms to choosing from a wide range of office types – Hi-tech centre at Hyderabad has it all. A preferred hub for most of Hyderabad’s prolific brands, IT companies, small and medium-sized businesses, this is a complete business ecosystem that offers end to end solutions. By being a part of the centre you can choose to collaborate with goal-oriented individuals from IT industries and the financial sector. Commuting is easy, thanks to well-connected roads and a metro station nearby.

  • Overview
  • Building Total Area (Including Basements) : 700,000 Sft.
  • Total leasable Area : Approx. 500,000 Sft.
  • No. of Floors : 4 Basements + Ground + 12 Floors
  • Average Floor Plate : Ground and First Floor- Approx. 33,574 Sft. Second floor till Twelfth Floor- Approx. 38,871 Sft.
  • Efficiency : Approx. 79%
  • Floor to Floor Height : Ground and First Floor- 4 Mts. Second Floor till Twelfth Floor- 3.4 Mts.
  • Car Parking Capacity : 500 Car Parks
  • Parking Ratio : Car park Density: 1 slot: 1000 Sft leased.
  • Power Allocation : 1 KVA for every 100 Sft.
  • Power Back up : 100 %
  • HVAC – VRV Systems : High Side AC- VRV Systems shall be provided as part of warm shell space
  • Fire Fighting Systems : Fire Hydrants and Sprinklers in common Area.
  • Elevators : 6 Nos. Passenger Elevators (20 Passengers capacity) 2 Nos. Service elevators (1400 Kgs capacity each)
