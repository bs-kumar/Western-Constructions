About Western Plaza

Luxurious 2 & 3 BHK Flats in Gated Community living at Hyderabad's premier residential location is brought to you by Western Constructions. Envisage a new experience of lavish living. Be a part of buzzing development, only a short walk from Jubilee Hills, a host of hospitals, schools, malls, restaurants, cafes & clubs. Flanked all around by green vibrancy and a pollution free zone, these healthy homes definitely provide the recipe for happy living. The Plaza is proud to provide a swimming pool and a club house for relaxing and socializing with the cream crowd in burgeoning, elite neighbourhood. Extra large rooms with individual balconies permit privileged viewing. 468 individual apartments of 2 & 3 BHK sizes with assured quality and modernistic architectural approach, the plaza is definitely a landmark venture.
